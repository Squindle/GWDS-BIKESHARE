How does our bikeshare scheme work?

Renting and Booking:

Well it's quite simple, if you need a bike, you get a bike!

Our easy renting system means that you can rent a bike in seconds with no fuss. Simply find a pick-up point and select a bike, give the QR code a scan and off you go! You can also book a bike in advance to make sure there is one waiting for you at a specific time whenever you need. Bikes can be booked up to a week in advance and cancelled instantly up until your booked time.

Bikes can be picked-up and dropped-off at the same point for any round trips. You can also pause the ride for up to 30 minutes which will lock the wheels of the bike, this means whether you need to pick up some take-out or pop to the shops, our bikes can help! We also offer day and 3 day long bookings for our bikes so intead of having to travel to a pick up point you can store it at home! With our SmartLock system any time the bike is locked it will be immobilised and tracked if it starts moving. We accept ApplePay, GooglePay and most major credit/debit cards*.

*American Express not accepted.
